Why this theme?

Stellenbosch Rugby is legendary, and the Norton family represents its generational spirit. The story of Chris and Riley Norton reflects more than just sporting success. it embodies teamwork, pride, and dedication. Rugby in Stellenbosch is deeply tied to community identity, making this theme both culturally significant and personally inspiring.

How information was gathered?

Through interviews, local articles, and community rugby records. We collected original photographs, personal observations, and field notes during matches and training sessions. Additional insights were drawn from official university archives.

Our evidence includes: locker room celebrations, candid photos of players, and interviews with teammates. This original content ensures authenticity and complies with project requirements.
